About

I’m a law student and independent "researcher" (i.e. I read stuff I find interesting and sometimes write other stuff about it). My formal focus is in private law Besides that I also enjoy reading about the most disparate subjects including but not limited to economics, history, neurology, philosophy and a few others.

I'm a Swiss national but I am currently residing in Italy for academic purposes, though given recent developments in the Swiss legal and social landscape the option of permanently switching my residence to Italy appears increasingly endearing.

Areas of interest

Private law, natural law theory, praxeology, modern and contemporary history, objectivism, psychometrics, psychedelics, etc.
